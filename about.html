Who we are

About ZMW Apps

ZMW Apps is an independent software company focused on building clean, practical tools for everyday people.

We build for people who take their hobbies seriously and want a better way to stay organized — without the complexity, subscriptions, or internet dependency that most apps require.

We believe software should get out of your way. No bloat, no dark patterns, no harvesting your data to sell ads. Just tools that do what they say and respect the people using them.

Our First Product

📦

Collectors App

A fully customizable app for tracking and managing your personal collections — figures, statues, LEGO sets, die-cast cars, and anything else you collect. Unlike most collection apps, Collectors App works completely offline, so your data stays on your device and you stay in control.

What We Stand For

Small and focused
